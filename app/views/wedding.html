We're Getting Married!

We're tying the knot in beautiful Interlaken, Switzerland and we would love for you to join us in the celebration!

Our Story

It all started with an introduction from our mutual friend (now Officiant!) Erik Redoute. Erik Redoute and Sarah met on their study abroad in Greece & Turkey and became the best of friends. When they returned from abroad, Redoute introduced Sarah to his roommate, Erik Sundberg, and the adventures began!

After months of friendship, we began dating on March 8th, 2011. After Sarah graduated from Michigan State University, we decided to move to Portland, Oregon to start a new adventure. This March marked our 6 year anniversary.

Engagement

In December of 2014, Erik set his sole New Years resolution: propose to Sarah within the following year. Nearly 12 months later, he popped the question with just a few days to spare.

On December 8th 2015, Erik told Sarah to pack an overnight bag for a surprise trip. They drove from Portland toward Mt. Hood, pulling off onto a side road that turned to a dirt road, and dead ended at a roaring river. In the dark, they crossed a rickety metal bridge with their luggage, and climbed a lit stairwell where a classic red Swedish cottage sat atop a hill. The next morning Erik brought Sarah breakfast in bed, but instead of food beneath the tray, it was a ring!

Venue & Location

Travel in general has always been important to us both. Switzerland was one of the first places we traveled to together, and one of the few places to which we have returned. Combined with Erik's familial ties to the Swiss area and the natural beauty of the landscape, there seemed to be no better place for our wedding!

The ceremony will take place at Schloss Oberhofen, a 13th century castle on the edge of Lake Thun. Guests will meet on the castle grounds at 4:30pm for a welcome champagne toast, with the ceremony following lakeside at 5:00pm. Dinner and drinks will follow at the Schloss Oberhofen Restaurant.

After dinner, a shuttle service will take all guests back to the Ringgenberg house where we can drink and dance for the rest of the evening!

Weather

The ceremony will take place outdoors (weather permitting). The weather in this area of Switzerland will range between mid-50's and low-70's (F) in temperature for this time of year.
